Cantilever

This is accommodates one car on the ground level and one on the first level. It has only one Post on one side of the system to make maneuverability of cars easy. The car on the ground level needs to be moved out before parking/removing the car on the raised Platform.

Features

  • 2 cars per unit
  • Lifting capacity up to 2000 Kgs. (Car Weight)
  • MS Frame with chequered Plate Platform.
  • Electro-Hydraulic with One Cylinder for Smooth & Noiseless Operation
  • 24V Low Tension 'Man Present' Wall Push Buttons with Extractable Key
  • Electromagnetic Safety Lock to prevent Unwanted Lowering of Platform
  • Maximum Limit Switch to prevent over shooting of Platform
  • Manual Lowering incase of Power Failure
  • Power Consumption up to 0.03 units (approx) per operation
