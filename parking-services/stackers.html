Stackers

This is a Two Level Parking system; where one car is parked on the ground level and one on the first level. The Car on the ground level needs to be moved out before parking / removing the car on the raised platform.

Features

  • 2 cars per unit
  • Lifting capacity up to 2000 Kgs. (Car Weight)
  • Hot Dipped Galvanized Steel Side Sections
  • Hot Dipped Galvanized Corrugated Sheet Platform
  • Electro-Hydraulic with One Cylinder for Smooth & Noiseless Operation
  • 24V Low Tension 'Man Present' Wall Push Buttons with Extractable Key
  • Electromagnetic Safety Lock to prevent Unwanted Lowering of Platform
  • Maximum Limit Switch to prevent over shooting of Platform
  • Manual Lowering in case of Power Failure
  • Power Consumption up to 0.03 units (approx) per operation
